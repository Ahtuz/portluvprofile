Why InFund?

We believe that everybody has at least one creative idea that could be turned into a successful business. All it takes is one little push and some right connections, then it could become one of the next biggest startup business. As mentioned above, we want to help connect starters to the right people.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the economic industry of Indonesia by facilitating connections between business starters and investors.

Goals

Our goal is to see a lot of startup companies funded and become successful businesses. We want people’s business ideas to be realized. With authorization from OJK, we hope to become one of Indonesia’s trusted startup investments platform.

Connections

Every single thing in our lives is connected in some form or another. Your toaster is connected to electricity, your sink is connected to the plumbing system, your phone is connected to the internet, and so on.

Our idea is inspired by these connections. For something to be useful, it has to be connected to the right entity. Connecting someone to the right people is essential in starting a brand new startup company. All it takes is the right medium of communication between two sides, and that’s what we strive to be.
